the Actors of the Movie

The movie *"Home Alone" (1990) made its cast iconic figures in the world of cinema. Macaulay Culkin, as Kevin, instantly became a global star and a symbol of Christmas adventures. Joe Pesci and Daniel Stern, playing the burglars Harry and Marv, delivered an unforgettable comedic duo. Catherine O'Hara and John Heard, as Kevin's parents, added warmth and family spirit to the story, while John Candy stood out as the charismatic musician helping Kate. The cast played a key role in the film's success, making it a timeless Christmas classic.
